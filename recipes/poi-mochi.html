ONO POI MOCHI

  • Cook Time: 1 hour
  • Servings: 30
  • Category: Mochi

Some Tips:

Make sure your oil is hot enough and that you have enough oil

Lay out a pan of paper towels because they cook decently fast

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 bag poi (fresh kalo would be 1lb)
  • 1 box mochiko flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • canola oil for frying

DIRECTIONS:

  • Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl (not including the frying oil)
  • Heat up the frying oil until it hits 365-375 degrees.
  • The frying oil should be in a deep bowl enough to cover the whole mochi ball
  • The smaller the ball, the easier it will be because you do not want it to stick to the bottom
  • If it does stick to the bottom, then let it cook and release itself
  • It will be golden brown when it is done then transfer to the pan with paper towel
