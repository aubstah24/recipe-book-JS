GROUND TURKEY BURGERS

  • Cook Time: 30mins
  • Servings: 4
  • Category: Dinner

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 pack of ground turkey
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • salt & pepper
  • 1/4 cup mayo
  • 2 minced garlic
  • Lettuce cups
  • Mushrooms
  • Asparagus

DIRECTIONS:

  • Mix with your hands, ground turkey, spices, mayo
  • Place oil and the garlic into a pan on high heat
  • Shape patties and place them onto the pan
  • Add in a little more oil
  • Use you thumb and press in the middle of the burgers
  • Let it cook for 3 minutes until the bottom crust is formed
  • Cook on the other side for 5-6 minutes until cooked through
  • Sauteed mushrooms and asparagus on the side to go with your burger
  • Serve on the lettuce cups with mayo or your choice of sauce/li>
