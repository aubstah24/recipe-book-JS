MATCHA LATTE BASE

  • Cook Time: 5mins
  • Servings: 1
  • Category: Drink

Some Tips:

You can mix in some hojicha powder to give it a sweeter taste.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 TSP honey
  • Ice
  • 44mL hot water
  • 1 TSP matcha

DIRECTIONS:

  • Shake together the ice and milk in a shaker bottle
  • Pour it into a serving cup with ice
  • Mix together the hot water and honey until dissolved
  • Add in the matcha powder untill there are no clumps
  • Pour Matcha mixture over the milk
