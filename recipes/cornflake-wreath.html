CORNFLAKE CHRISTMAS WREATHS

  • Cook Time: 30mins
  • Servings: 12
  • Category: Snack/Dessert

Some Tips:

You can shape this however you want -- 1 big wreath or individual wreaths

For individual wreaths, use a donut pan to help shape it faster

INGREDIENTS:

  • 4 TBSP butter
  • 1 bag mini marshmallows
  • Green Food coloring
  • 5 cups cornflake cereal

DIRECTIONS:

  • Melt the butter in a saucepan
  • Add in the bag of marshmallows abd stir until everything is melted
  • Add green food coloring until you have the color you desire
  • Take saucepan off the heat and add in the cornflakes
  • Assemble with your hands sprayed with oil
  • Place in fridge to mold it
