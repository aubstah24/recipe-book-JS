UBE MOCHI BREAD

  • Cook Time: 4 hours
  • Servings: 20
  • Category: Bread

INGREDIENTS:

  • 270g Mochi bread mix
  • 35g sugar
  • 15g ube powder
  • 1 egg
  • 120mL of water
  • 25g unsalted butter (melted)
  • 100g mochiko flour
  • 30g corn starch
  • 50g sugar
  • 160mL water
  • 1 TSP oil

DIRECTIONS:

  • Mix together the mochi bread mix, sugar, and ube powder
  • Knead in the egg and water until it is smooth (~20mins)
  • Add in the melted butter then knead again (don't over knead)
  • Cover the bowl with a damp towel and rest for 1 hour to rise
  • Divide into small bit size pieces and cover with a towel again to rise for 15 more minutes
  • In another bowl, mix together the mochiko flour, corn starch, sugar, and water
  • Then add in the oil and mix well
  • Steam for 20 mins then let it cool
  • Knead it with plastic wrap then divide the mochi into the same number of pieces as the bread
  • Layer the bread then the mochi then the chocolate inside and seal well
  • Cover with a damp towel and let it rise for an hour
  • Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  • Lay out the balls on a baking sheet and cut a cross mark in each one then bake for 15 minutes
